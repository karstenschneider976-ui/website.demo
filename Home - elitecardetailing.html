my Process

Full Detailing Process
And Treatment

1. Remove trash, personal belongings, and car mats

We will remove trash, personal belongings, food wrappers etc.

2. Wheel and Tire Deep Clean

We will pre rinse and spray wheel cleaner on your wheels and tires and give it that nice shine it deserves, and we will treat it with special brushes and cleaners

3. Sit and Dwell

We let the soap and non harmful chemicals sit for about 40 seconds then we rinse the wheels

4. Pre rinse and Foam

We pre rinse your vehicle with a pressure washer. We don't get to close to the car and rip the paint. Then we apply a thick layer of foam to apply the soap to the car. Then get a microfiber wash-mit and hand wash the entire vehicle. We dig deep into every little crevice of the exterior of the car.

5. Drying Process

We dry the whole car with a microfiber towel rinsing the car in seconds with 1 towel and add some shine.
